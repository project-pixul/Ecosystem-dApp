How do I buy Pixul Token?

Pixul is launched on the Binance Smart Chain. This means that in order to buy Pixul, you need the Binance Coin (BNB). You can buy BNB from Trustwallet, Binance Smart Chain wallet, or from an exchange such as Binance itself, Crypto.com, Whitebit, or similar. Once you have BNB, send them to your wallet and head over to PancakeSwap to buy Pixul for BNB. Make sure you set your slippage to 8%.

How do I stake?

Go to our "Staking" page. It will automatically ask you to connect your wallet, connect the wallet that contains your Pixul Tokens. You will see your wallet information appear and the total amount of Pixul Tokens that you own in your wallet. We do not charge any fees for staking but you have to have BNB in your wallet in order to pay for the Binance SmartChain gas fees. Make sure you keep enough BNB in your wallet to claim or unstake you Pixul Tokens in the future. When you are all set use the slidebar to select how many Pixul Tokens you wish to stake. Press the "Stake" button to stake the amount of Pixul tokens you entered. Now your Pixul Tokens are staked and you'll earn 5% APY.

How do I claim my rewards?

When you have staked your Pixul Tokens you're able to claim your rewards anytime you wish. Just press the "Claim" button in our "Staking" page and your rewards will be automatically transferred to your wallet. Make sure you have enough BNB in your wallet to pay for the gas fees.

How do I unstake my Pixul Tokens?

Go to our "Staking" page and press the "Unstake" button. All of your staked Pixul Tokens and your rewards will be automatically transferred to your wallet. Make sure you have enough BNB in your wallet to pay for the gas fees.

Why is the price in my Trustwallet sometimes incorrect?

Trustwallet derives its pricing from CoinMarketCap's embedded API, and this service has been known to have incorrect and inflated pricing on smaller tokens. One reason for this is that they don't update the price as frequently in order to save resources.
